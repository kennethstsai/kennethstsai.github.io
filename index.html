What is City Squares?

As the urban planner of an up-and-coming city, it is your job to develop this fledgling town into the greatest settlement in the region. Outpace your rivals as you construct infrastructure and draft policies, but be careful not to let your city's pollution grow out of hand!

How to Play

1. Give each player one City Mat and two Token Trackers. Each player starts with 3 resources and zero pollution.

2. Separate the cards by type. Energy cards have a green banner, Infrastructure cards have an orange banner, and Policy cards have a purple banner.

3. Randomly remove 15 Infrastructure Cards and 10 Policy Cards. These cards will not be used during the game.

4. Shuffle the remaining 60 cards into a single deck, and deal each player a starting hand of three cards.

5. Place the remaining deck face-down in the center of the play area. Reveal the top three cards, and place them face-up to the right of the deck.

6. Players may now choose to discard their starting hand and draw a new hand of two cards from the deck. Each player may only do so once.

7. Set the Die in the center of the play area, and set it to 1. The die is used to track the number of rounds elapsed.

8. You're now ready to play!

Card Types

Click on a card to learn more!

Energy Cards: The standard building type in City Squares. Energy cards produce a variable amount of resources and pollution, and have no secondary effects.

Infrastructure Cards: These buildings have special effects alongside their normal resource and pollution production. Some Infrastructure cards have an immediate effect, but others are ongoing.

Policy Cards: These cards allow you to perform special actions at any time, but do not remain in your city and don't count towards your final score. Use them to get a leg up on the competition!
